# Art Guide

## Visual Direction
Crystal Hunter uses a bold, neon, high-contrast style that fits a web-based fantasy action game.

## Color Palette
- Deep space blue background
- Cyan and emerald highlights for crystals and friendly effects
- Red and orange enemy tones
- Gold for rewards and important UI elements

## Visual Principles
- Readable silhouettes
- Strong separation between player, enemies, and objects
- Clear feedback for impacts and rewards
