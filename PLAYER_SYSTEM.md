# Player System

## Player Attributes
- HP, Mana, and Stamina
- Movement speed and dash behavior
- Attack damage, crit chance, and range
- Inventory and progression stats

## Progression
Players gain experience from enemy kills and level up over time. Each level increases survivability and offensive power.

## Equipment and Customization
- Weapon tiers affect damage and attack cadence
- Armor influences survivability
- Skins change visual identity and can be purchased in the shop
