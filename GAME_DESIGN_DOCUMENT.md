# Game Design Document

## Overview
Crystal Hunter RPG is a top-down action experience where the player explores a fractured world, defeats enemies, collects crystals, and spends rewards on upgrades.

## Core Loop
1. Move through the arena or dungeon.
2. Defeat waves of enemies.
3. Collect crystals and gold.
4. Spend gold on upgrades or cosmetics.
5. Survive long enough to clear waves and reach boss encounters.

## Player Fantasy
The player is a wandering crystal hunter who grows stronger as they gather relic energy and uncover the secrets of the world.

## Progression Systems
- Experience and leveling
- Stat upgrades for HP, speed, and damage
- Weapon and skin progression
- Quests and achievements

## Economy
- Gold earned from enemy kills and crystals
- Crystals used as collectible rewards and inventory tracking
- Shop purchases improve survivability and offense

## Tone
- Fast-paced and readable
- Stylized, neon, high-contrast visuals
- Lightweight fantasy with arcade feedback
