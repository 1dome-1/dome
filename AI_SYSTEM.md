# AI System

## Current Implementation
Enemy AI is lightweight and rule-based. Enemies chase the player when in range and otherwise wander in a simple pattern.

## Behavior Design
- Normal enemies: chase and attack when close
- Elite enemies: stronger and more aggressive
- Boss enemies: larger health pool and wider aggro range

## Future Direction
The AI system can be expanded with behavior states such as retreat, patrol, ambush, and phase-based boss logic.
