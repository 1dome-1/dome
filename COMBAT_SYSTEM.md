# Combat System

## Core Mechanics
- Melee attack with mouse aim and combo scaling
- Projectile shot for ranged pressure
- Skill blast for area damage
- Dash for mobility and evasion
- Invulnerability and hit flash feedback

## Combat Loop
1. Player moves with keyboard or pointer input.
2. Attacks damage nearby enemies.
3. Projectiles and skill blasts apply extra pressure.
4. Enemies retaliate and reduce HP.
5. Defeated enemies grant gold, experience, and crystals.

## Balance Notes
Combat currently favors readability and momentum over deep tactical complexity.
